FAQs

1. What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of home services, including TV, AC, Laptop, Refrigerator, Water Purifier, and Washing Machine repair, as well as plumbing, electrician, and carpentry services.


2. How do I book a service?

You can easily book a service through our website or mobile app. Simply select the service you need, choose a convenient time, and provide your contact details.


3. Are your technicians certified?

Yes, all our technicians are certified professionals with years of experience in their respective fields. We ensure that each technician undergoes thorough background checks and training.


4. How much do your services cost?

Our pricing is transparent and competitive. You can view the estimated cost for each service on our website before booking. Final pricing may vary based on the specific requirements of the job.


5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.


6. What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 4 hours before the scheduled service time. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.


7. Is there a warranty on the services provided?

Yes, we offer a warranty on most of our repair services. The warranty period varies depending on the type of service. Please check the specific service details for more information.


8. Do you use genuine parts for repairs?

We use only high-quality, genuine parts for all repairs to ensure the longevity and proper functioning of your appliances.


9. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via phone, email, or live chat. Contact Us page for more details.


10. How long will the service take?

The duration of the service depends on the type and complexity of the job. Most repair services are completed within a few hours. Our technician will provide an estimated time after assessing the issue.


11. What if I am not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not happy with the service provided, please contact our customer support team, and we will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
