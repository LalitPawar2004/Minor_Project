Privacy policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Home Care! This website is developed as part of a college minor project to simulate a home service booking platform. We understand the importance of your privacy and are committed to handling your personal information with care.


2. Information We Collect

• Personal Information:

When you interact with our website, such as when booking a service or contacting us, we may collect basic personal information like your name, email address, phone number, and address.


• Payment Information:

If applicable, we might collect payment-related details to simulate transactions as part of the project. Please note that any payment information collected is for educational purposes only and is not used for real transactions.


• Usage Data:

We automatically collect data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and the pages you visit on our website. This information helps us understand how users interact with our site and improve its functionality.


3. Purpose of Data Collection

• Service Simulation:

The personal information collected is used to simulate booking and managing home services as part of this educational project.


• Communication:

We use your contact information to send simulated service confirmations, updates, and respond to inquiries.


• Project Analysis:

Data collected during your visit may be used for analyzing the effectiveness of our website design, features, and user interaction as part of our project assessment..


• Educational Use:

information collected is used solely for educational purposes and not for commercial use.


4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your personal information. Basic security measures have been implemented to safeguard your data; however, please be aware that this is a student project, and the security measures may not be as robust as those on commercial platforms.


5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and providing personalized content. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.


6. Use of Third-Party Services

• Analytics:

We may use third-party tools like Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. These tools may collect data about your device and browsing activities.


• Payment Processing:

If our project includes a payment simulation, we might use third-party payment processors. However, no real transactions will be processed.


7. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes. Data may be shared with our instructors or project evaluators strictly for educational evaluation purposes.


8. Your Rights

• Access and Correction:

You have the right to access and update your personal information by contacting us directly.


• Opt-Out:

You can opt out of receiving any communications related to the project by contacting us.


• Data Deletion:

You may request the deletion of your personal information from our records at any time. Since this is a project, we will delete all data after the project concludes.


9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

As this project evolves, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or requirements. Any changes will be communicated through our website, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.


10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at: homecare@gmail.com


11. Project Disclaimer

Please note that this website is a student project for educational purposes only. The services, transactions, and data handling practices simulated on this website are not for real commercial use.
